    About Cacau Show Chocolates

    Cacau Show is a Brazilian chocolate manufacturer and retailer. Founded in 1988, the company is the largest chocolate franchise in the world and has over 2,000 stores in Brazil and abroad. Cacau Show is known for its high-quality chocolates and creative flavors.

    Whether you're looking for a special gift or just want to indulge your sweet tooth, Cacau Show has a wide range of chocolates to choose from, including truffles, bars, and pralines. Their products are made with premium ingredients and are available in a variety of flavors, such as dark chocolate, milk chocolate, white chocolate, and more.
